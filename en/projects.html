Shadorian is my musical project that I started in 2020, mostly during the lockdown. I'm an electro artist who do House, Cyberpunk, Midtempo, Techno and Bass music - here is an exemple of what it sounds like -.
I also do remixes of pop and rap songs i like, just for fun.

The universe of Shadorian is a cyberpunk world, where technology is everywhere, the population has concentrated in huge cities with skyscrapers as far as the eye can see, and neon lights everywhere. I love this aesthetic and it inspire me in my tracks.

For the visuals, I use my skills in Blender to create the scenes I want in the m/v. They are still in work, it takes a lot of time, but as I'm getting better at Blender, it's going faster.

3D MODELLING

My journey on Blender started in 2020 when my best friend showed me her isometric houses. I was amazed by the render of it and wanted to do the same. I instantly started with a isometric house, following a tutorial but i realised it was to hard for a beginning.
So i searched for beginners tutorial and found the tutorial every person went by : Blender Tutorial for Complete Beginners by Blender Guru aka the Donut tutorial. I never finished it (i should...), tried other tutorials and started making my own renders. Here is a gallery regrouping all my final renders and tries.

Personal Projects

I also work on personal projects in my free time, just for fun. Here are some of them.

I've tried several times to make a video game, but I never managed to finish anything — I always gave up in the middle of the process. The reason is that I lacked time and my ideas were a bit too ambitious, haha!
