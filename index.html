ABOUT ME

Hello !
My name is Estevan ROLLAND,
I'm 19 years old and I'm a first year computer science student in Nantes, France.
My goal is to get an ingeneer diploma in computer science, specialised in software and application development.

Passionate about music and digital arts like 3D modelling, I developed a real creative mind. I played piano for 12 years and started creating my own tracks in 2020.
My artist name is Shadorian, I make electro music, mostly synthwave, techno and house. I sometimes release remixes of songs I like on my YouTube channel and Soundcloud.

As a fan of movies, especially sci-fi movies, I always wanted to try to recreate the scenes I saw in Star Wars, Marvel's movies, but also video games like Cyberpunk 2077.
So I decided to learn Blender to start my journey into 3D modelling. I already had the basics and a good sense of space thanks to my little experience with SketchUp and Solidworks.
I like to draw, whether it's for my projects or just for fun. I absolutely love the cyberpunk aesthetic, the way it combines dystopia and technology: big cities with skyscrapers
as far as the eye can see, and neon lights everywhere.

Curious about languages and cultures, I enjoy learning new languages and researching their art, customs and history. There is always something to learn from others !
I am fluent in French and English, I learned German at school and have started to learn Spanish, Italian, Korean, Chinese and Arabic on my own.

Although I'm an introvert, I'm social and enjoy meeting new people, but I also value spending time with my friends and family.
I love to travel. It allows me to discover new places, new cultures and new people who may share the same passions as me.

On the professional side, I am a serious and hardworking person, able to work alone as well as in a team. Working under pressure is not a problem, I know how to manage
my stress and keep calm in difficult times.
I just know that my dream job would be somewhere where they blend development, 3D modelling and music together. I really love being able to express my creativity
while helping others, so that's a real professional goal of mine.

Currently, I have a few projects that combine my hobbies like creating a FPS multiplayer videogame or visuals for my music.
Some of these projects have been reported due to my lack of skills at the moment of development.
